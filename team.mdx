## Problem that Embedchain is solving

Embedchain is building a data platform for LLMs. The platform makes it easier for anyone to create LLM apps on top of their unstructured data, which keeps on getting updated. Behind the hood, the platform abstracts out the entire process of loading, indexing, retrieving and syncing the data and expose easy to use apis.
